Escape the Loop by Mozilla Club of UCSC

We urge you to carefully read the terms and conditions of this page before entering the contest.


Contest Name: Escape the Loop Hackathon

Entry Period: The Contest begins on October 22, 2020 at 00:00 AM (GMT +5.30) and ends on October 26, 2020 at 11:59 PM.

How to Enter: All entries must be submitted no later than the end of the Entry Period. You may enter the Contest during the Entry Period as follows:
  1. Visit the Contest webpage part of the DEV Community Site located here.
  2. Follow any instructions on the Contest Page and submit your completed entry (each an "Entry").
  3. After publishing the post on Dev Community, the participants must fill the form on Escape the Loop official website as well.
  4. All the teams should participate in the pitching session which will be held on October 26, 2020 to October 28, 2020. Any team who fails to participate will be disqualified.
  • A Team should consist of no more than five (05) members and all the members of the team should be undergraduates of University of Colombo School of Computing.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.


Required Elements for Entries: Each Entry must include, at a minimum, the following elements:

  1. A published submission post on DEV in the `#escapetheloop2020` tag (we encourage you to use this template).
  2. The post must include links to your demo and code repository.
  3. The code repository, the project must include a README and an open source license.

The judges' decision is final and no correspondence will be entered into.
